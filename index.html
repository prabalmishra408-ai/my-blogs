Executive Summary Asian Paints maintained robust growth over the last two years, consistently gaining market share and demonstrating resilience despite raw material volatility and uncertain demand in some segments. Management’s focus has shifted toward expanding decorative and industrial business, deepening presence across rural and premium urban markets, and leveraging digital initiatives. Company Overview - Sector: Decorative and industrial paints, home improvement. - Market Position: Market leader in India, significant global presence across 15+ countries. - Key Strengths: Wide distribution, strong brand, continuous product innovation, and digital transformation. Recent Performance Highlights - Strong revenue growth year-over-year, reflecting steady demand. - EBITDA margin improvement, driven by lower input costs. - Notable net profit increases as margins expanded. - Volume growth in double digits for the last two fiscal years. Recent Trends & Insights from Last 8 Concall Summaries - Growth has been volume-led, driven by sales in smaller cities and ongoing market share gains from unorganized sector players. - Margins have improved due to easing input costs, but the management remains cautious as raw materials show signs of volatility again this year. - Premium product ranges continue to gain traction, with further emphasis on value-added product lines and Home Décor. - Home Décor and adjacent businesses are contributing a noticeable share of consolidated revenue, with ambitions to scale, though margins face pressure from early-phase investments and rising competition. - Industrial and OEM paint segments experienced a rebound, especially in automotive and infrastructure-related demand. - Continued investments in digital initiatives and significant expansion in the rural dealer network. - Major capex underway in plant expansions at Khandala and Mysuru, with management maintaining a positive long-term demand outlook. - Risks highlighted include potential raw material inflation, intensifying competition, and fluctuations in real estate market activity. Analyst’s View Asian Paints remains the industry leader thanks to a broad portfolio, strong brand equity, and wide distribution. The outlook is positive for volume-driven growth, though future margins could face pressure if raw material costs increase. The company’s execution risk is low, but the competitive landscape is evolving quickly, especially with new entrants investing heavily in decorative paints. Home Décor will be a focus area for monitoring, as it holds growth potential but currently weighs on margins. Conclusion: - Outlook: Positive on sustained revenue growth; cautiously optimistic on margins depending on raw material stability. - Key Watch Points: Trends in input prices, progress in the Home Décor division, and the pace of competitor expansion.
